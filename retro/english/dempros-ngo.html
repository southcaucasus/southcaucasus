DEMPROS NGO, ARMENIA

THE GOALS OF THE ORGANIZATION

- to promote the program of Armenia's membership to the EU and NATO,

- to promote and be an active initiator in the affair of raising the long-term and mutually beneficial military-political and economic cooperation between Armenia and the USA to a qualitatively new level,

- to promote the inclusion programs of the three countries of the South Caucasus in the same military-political and economic systems,

- to promote the formation and the development of democratic institutions and the formation of civil society,

- to promote the formation of public, state governmental, local self-administration systems of the republic, the improvement of market relations and the formation of new viable systems,

- to promote the establishment of intensive political, security, economic, cultural relations with the EU and NATO,

- to promote the strengthening of regional cooperation, the activation of the society's participation in the affair of conflict prevention and regulation,

- to promote and to be an active initiator in those processes the aim of which is to establish horizontal relations between the three countries of the South Caucasus within the scope of "Individual Partnership" with NATO and the program "New Neighbors" with the EU in the form of many-sided cooperation of the South Caucasus,

- to promote the raising of the involvement level of RA in the affair of guaranteeing peace and security in the region,

- to promote the processes of preservation of the national identity of the Armenian people in the motherland and Diaspora, the comprehensive unification of Diaspora's potential, the development of factors securing the longevity of the national spiritual heritage and the factors which embody the national identity, preserving the bases of originality, the adoption of universal cultural values and achievements, the many-sided development of national culture,

- to promote democracy's development, the defense of man's and citizen's rights and basic liberties, to contribute to the notification of the Armenian public to Western democratic and civilization values and making the society immediately concerned in those processes,

- to promote the creation of an atmosphere of trust between the societies of the three countries of the South Caucasus, making them jointly concerned in regional, geopolitical, various political and economic programs and the establishment of partnership relations.
